Oscars

You can find out the Oscar Nominations or Nominees using the form below.
For Nominations you can narrow results by year, category, nominee, info and whether or not they won.
Alternatively you can include a restriction in the Nominee or info box which will check if your criteria can be found in either the nominee or info sections.
If you use the Nominee or info box, please leave both the Nominee and Info boxes empty.
To get Nominees, click on the Nominees button, you can narrow results by whether or not they won and how many times.
